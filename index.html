Welcome!

First Hyderabad Book Fair was held in the year 1985 at City Central Library, Ashok Nagar, Hyderabad. A few important publishers and Book sellers participated in the fair. There was enourmous response from the book lovers and the people of Hyderabad. Later on the Book Fairs were held at Nizam College Grounds, Public Gardens, Exhibition Grounds, Keshav Memorial High School Ground etc. From the last four year onwards it has been continiously running at Telangana Kala Bharati (NTR Grounds). With in a short period of time Hyderabad Book Fair has become very popular through out India.
