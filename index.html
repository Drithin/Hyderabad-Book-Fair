Welcome!

First Hyderabad Book Fair was held in the year 1985 at City Central Library, Ashok Nagar, Hyderabad. A few essential publishers and Booksellers participated in the fair. There was an enormous response from the book lovers and the people of Hyderabad. Later on, the Book Fairs were held at Nizam College Grounds, Public Gardens, Exhibition Grounds, Keshav Memorial High School Ground etc. From the last four year onwards it has been continuously running at Telangana Kala Bharati (NTR Grounds). Within a short time Hyderabad Book Fair has become very popular throughout India.

Bala Mela

Hyderabad Book Fair(HBF) believes that apart from the academic milestones, it is essential for a child to experience Literature and Arts. HBF organises a large number of activities for children such a Painting Competition, Fancy Dress, Mimicry, Dance Competitions and Mono Acting. 10,000+ students participated in the Cultural activities conducted by Hyderabad Book Fair 2019-2020

Literary

Literary Programs are exclusively organized at Nomula Satyanarayana Vedika. It is an assembly of Poets, Writers, Book Authors of distinct genres come together to discuss and analyze different literary works. Prachina Padya Sourabham, Sablatarn Sahityam and Cinima Kalatmakata are some of the programs organized at Hyderabad Book Fair 2019-2020

Book Releases

Hyderabad Book Fair organizes a Book Releases at C Raghavachari Vedika, an exclusive arena dedicated to new and veteran authors to release their books. Books like Sadalakshmi, Dismantling J&K, Moosi Nadhi Matladutunnadi, Neeti Needa and Maha Bharatam Mana Charitra are some of manyu books that were released at HBF 2019-2020.
